Pipes

A pipe is a long, round, hollow object,
usually through which a liquid or gas can flow.

Bars

a metal, which is cast in the shape
of a block for convenient handling.

Sheets

any metal that can be formed into
flat pieces of varying thicknesses.

Flanges

a method of connecting pipes, valves, pumps
and other equipment to form a piping system

Fasteners

used to join multiple components, whether they're
made from sheet metal, plastics or some other material.

Buttweld-Fittings

a weldable pipe fitting that allows for change
of direction of flow, to branch off, reduce pipe size or
attach auxiliary equipment
